Where is pollstr useful?

Back Office with no User Interface: internal processes to increase productivity.

Automate everyday processes seamessly into the workflows that you already know.

Back Office with User Interface: internal processes to increase productivity.

Automate processes using an interface for interaction, for example: a chatbot.

Front Office: processes for your customers.

Automate processes for your customers within your website or on social media.
